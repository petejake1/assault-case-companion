# Assault Case Companion

**The World's Most Advanced Victim Rights & Assault Case Toolkit**

Built directly from the complete methods, templates, strategies, and document systems of the 2025 Zorbaz Pelican Lake Unprovoked Chokehold Assault Project (State v. Studsrud / Wagner cases + full civil, POST, OLPR, CVJU, Human Rights, and demand package).

## What It Does

1. **Smart Case Intake Wizard** – Capture every critical detail (incident, parties, injuries, LE misconduct, victim rights violations, medical, evidence, criminal histories).
2. **One-Click Professional Document Generation** – Instantly produce:
   - Civil Demand / Settlement Letters (Dram Shop + Battery + Premises)
   - Point-by-Point Cease-and-Desist Rebuttals
   - POST Board Misconduct Complaints (Deputy-level)
   - OLPR Ethical Complaints (Prosecutors)
   - Minnesota Crime Victim Rights Act (§ 611A) Complaints
   - MN Department of Human Rights Complaints
   - Letters to the Court / Judge re: Victim Rights Violations
   - Board of Commissioners Oversight Requests
   - Master Evidence Summaries & Key Facts Highlights
   - Settlement Value Analysis & Demand Strategy
3. **Live Tracking Dashboard** – Hearings, filings, deadlines, status of every complaint and demand.
4. **Evidence Vault** – Inventory + notes for video, bodycam, medical, criminal records.
5. **Local-First & Private** – Everything stored in your browser (localStorage). No servers. Export full case packages as ZIP of Markdown/HTML ready for Word/PDF.
6. **Demo Mode** – Load the full anonymized 2025 Zorbaz project structure as a living template.

## Tech Stack
- Next.js 14 (App Router)
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS (navy + gold legal aesthetic)
- Zustand (state)
- Client-side only (Vercel edge ready)
- JSZip + FileSaver for package export
